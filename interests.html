Books I Like

I find reading really enjoyable. When I pick up a book, I get to explore new ideas, meet fascinating characters, and go on epic adventures, from the comfort of my own home. It's a great way to relax and unwind after a hectic day. Whether I'm in the mood for something that will force me to reevaluate everything I've ever learned or something that's a warm, cozy escape from reality, reading never fails to entertain and inspire me. Music I Like

Music is similar to reading in that it can be a perfect escape from reality. I love music that feels raw and real, like something personal and revealing that the artist has created. It helps me understand myself better and it’s really good at creating an atmosphere, whether I’m studying, on the car, or resting after school. Above are four albums that I love to listen to (Collapsed in Sunbeams by Arlo Parks, The Record by Boygenius, Jubilee by Japanese Breakfast, and Antisocialites by Alvvays).
